About

Taylor Raynor is a Florida-based songwriter, musician and performer. Borrowing from folk, indie and pop music, Raynor has found a unique style of his own.

Taylor’s debut LP “The Story of You and Me” is a concept album that depicts the ups and downs of a romantic relationship. The songs range from simple quirky love songs with a hint of pop to more somber acoustic songs. Raynor is currently promoting and touring his album and will have more to come.
